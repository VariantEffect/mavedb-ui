How MaveMD works
From variant identifier to clinical evidence in three steps
1
Enter your variant
Search by HGVS, ClinGen Allele ID, dbSNP rsID, or ClinVar Variation ID. MaveMD resolves identifiers via the ClinGen Allele Registry and maps coordinates across GRCh37 and GRCh38.
→
2
Match MAVE measurements
MaveMD identifies all matching variant effect measurements across MaveDB, including nucleotide and amino acid variants linked via MANE transcripts.
→
3
Apply ACMG/AMP evidence
Calibrated score sets provide functional evidence codes — BS3 or PS3 — compatible with ACMG/AMP variant classification guidelines.

Idea 4

Empty States & 404

Faded logo bars serve as a visual anchor for empty and error states, making them feel on-brand instead of generic. The bars are "empty" — reflecting the absence of data.

Idea 5

Card Corner Accents

Subtle branded accents on detail cards — just enough to reinforce the palette without competing with the content. Four variants at different levels of subtlety.

A — Left Bar Accent
A sage-to-mint gradient bar runs along the left edge of the card, borrowing from the vertical bars in the logo. Subtle but distinctive.
B — Top Rainbow Strip
A thin gradient strip across the top edge uses the full palette sweep (sage → mint → yellow → orange), echoing the logo's colour sequence.
C — Corner Fade
A very subtle sage triangle in the top-right corner. The lightest touch — almost invisible until you look for it. Good for detail cards where content is dense.
D — Bottom Mini-Bars
Tiny horizontal bars at the card bottom in the palette colours, like a miniature version of the logo's histogram. A subtle branded footer.

What's new

Feb 2026 New feature

Calibration scores now available

Score sets can now include calibrated pathogenicity evidence alongside raw functional scores, enabling direct comparison across assays.

Jan 2026 New feature

IGVF data linkages

MaveDB score sets are now linked to IGVF data, enabling cross-resource variant effect comparisons.

Dec 2025 Improvement

Variant search expanded to support ClinGen CAId

You can now look up variants using ClinGen Canonical Allele Identifiers in addition to HGVS, dbSNP, and ClinVar IDs.
